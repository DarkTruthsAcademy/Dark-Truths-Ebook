Chapter 6: Production Workflow Mastery

You don't need expensive equipment or editing skills. You need the right workflow.

The 30-Minute Video Creation System

Research (10 minutes)

Use Wikipedia, Reddit's r/UnresolvedMysteries, historical databases, and declassified documents. Look for stories with conflict, injustice, or hidden connections.

Script (5 minutes)

Write your hook first. Everything else supports the hook. Aim for 150-200 words (40-50 second video). Use short sentences. Create rhythm.

Visuals (10 minutes)

Use AI image generators (Midjourney, Leonardo.ai) for atmospheric visuals. Combine with historical photos from public domain archives. Add subtle zoom/pan effects.

Edit (5 minutes)

Use CapCut templates. Add text overlays with bold, high-contrast fonts. Include particle effects or light leaks for atmosphere. Keep transitions snappy.
